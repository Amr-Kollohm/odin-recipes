Asab Recipe

Description

The go to refreshing and energy-giving Egyptian street drink. While Asab can technically be homemade, it is more often ordered through street vendors.

If drunk fresh, Asab is the best 5 Pounds you're ever going to spend.

Ingredients

  • Sugarcane
  • Lime
  • Ice

Steps

  1. Squeeze the sugarcane (Usually done with special machinery)
  2. Put the sugarcane juice in a container filled with ice
  3. Top off with a squeeze of lime and enjoy
