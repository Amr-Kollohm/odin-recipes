Koshary Recipe

Description

Egypt's number 1 national dish and widely popular street food. The Egyptian's Pizza, if you will. Koshary was sold on food carts in its early years, and was introduced to restaurants later.

Koshary is not a fancy dish by any means, it traditionally does not contain any animal products, making it vegan and cheap and therefore widely popular among workers and laborers.

Main Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Brown Lentils
  • Macaroni
  • Onions
  • Tomato Sauce

Recommended Additions

  • Chili Oil
  • Garlic Vinegar (Da'aa)
  • Chickpeas
  • Short Spaghetti

Steps

  1. Make Rice
  2. Make tomato sauce
  3. Slice onion into thin rings
  4. Fry onions till brown and crispy
  5. Boil brown Lentils
  6. Put it all together
